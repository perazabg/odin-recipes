Tamales

About Dish

Tamales are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of prepared corn masa that is filled with delicious fillings and wrapped in corn husks or banana leaves before being steamed. They can be found in many different sizes and flavors

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Total Time: 2 hrs

Servings: 15 tamales

Ingredients

  • 30 Corn Husks for Tamales
Filling
  • 1 Russet Potato Diced
  • 2 Carrots Diced
  • 2 Chicken Breasts Cooked and Shredded
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1/4 Onion julienned
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
Masa
  • 2.5 cups Masa for Tamales
  • 1.25 cups Chicken Broth
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 8 oz pork lard
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
